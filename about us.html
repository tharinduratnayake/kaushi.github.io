About 999 Hospital

Welcome to 999 Hospital, a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing exceptional medical care across multiple locations in Sri Lanka. With branches in Colombo, Kandy , and Batticaloa, we are committed to delivering comprehensive, patient-centered healthcare services to communities throughout the country.

Our Mission

At 999 Hospital, our mission is to enhance the health and well-being of every individual we serve. We believe in providing high-quality, accessible healthcare that meets the diverse needs of our patients, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Branches

Colombo Branch

Modern services are provided by our Colombo branch, which includes obstetrics, cardiology, and general medicine among other medical specialties. This branch, which is in the center of the city, is manned by highly qualified individuals who are committed to your care and is furnished with cutting-edge technology.

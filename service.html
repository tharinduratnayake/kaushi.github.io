Comprehensive Medical Services at 999 Hospital

Emergency Services

24/7 Emergency Room (ER) services, Trauma care, Immediate treatment for acute conditions (e.g., heart attack, stroke).

Inpatient Care

General Medicine wards, Surgical wards, Specialized care units (e.g., cardiology, neurology), Intensive Care Unit (ICU), High Dependency Unit (HDU).

Surgical Services

General surgery, Specialized surgeries (e.g., orthopedic, cardiovascular, neurosurgery), Day surgeries and procedures.

Diagnostic and Imaging Services

Laboratory services (e.g., blood tests, urine tests), Radiology (e.g., X-ray, MRI, CT scan), Ultrasound, Endoscopy.

Pharmacy Services

In-house pharmacy for medication dispensing, Medication counseling, Prescription refills.

Mental Health Services

Psychiatry and counseling, Addiction treatment programs, Stress management and therapy.

Ambulatory Care

Day care procedures, Ambulatory surgery, Minor outpatient treatments, Telemedicine services.

Remote Consultations

Online follow-ups, Virtual health monitoring.
